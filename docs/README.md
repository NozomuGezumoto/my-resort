## Privacy Policy (Required for Stores)

App Store and Google Play require a **privacy policy URL**.

### Publishing Options

1. **GitHub Pages**
   - Settings → Pages → deploy from `docs/`
   - URL: `https://[username].github.io/my-resort/privacy-policy.html`

2. **Your website**
   - Host `privacy-policy.html` on your server

3. **Notion / Google Sites**
   - Copy content from PRIVACY_POLICY.md

### Before Submitting

- Replace `[Please set your contact email]` with your email in PRIVACY_POLICY.md
- Replace `[YOUR_USERNAME]` in APP_STORE.md with your GitHub username
