Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 5, 2025

My Resort Map ("the App") is committed to respecting your privacy and being transparent about how we handle personal information.

1. Introduction

The App helps you discover resort beaches on a map and record your visits and memories. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App collects, how it is used, and your rights.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information We Do NOT Collect

The App does not collect:

  • Name, email address, phone number
  • Account information (the App has no login feature)
  • Precise location (GPS)
  • Contacts, payment information, or other personally identifiable information

2.2 Information Stored Locally on Your Device

The following information you enter or select is stored only on your device. It is never sent to our servers or third parties.

  • Visited / Want-to-go lists
  • Memos, photo references, ratings
  • Filter settings

3. Permissions We Use

Photo Library: Used only when you tap "Add photo" to add photos to your resort memories. Photos are stored only on your device. We do not use location (GPS).

4. Third-Party Services

We use Apple Map Kit (iOS) and Google Maps (Android) for map display. We do not use analytics, advertising, or tracking.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

All data exists only on your device. Uninstalling the App deletes it.
