# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated**: March 5, 2025

My Resort Map (the "App") is committed to respecting your privacy and being transparent about how we handle personal information.

---

## 1. Introduction

My Resort Map is an app that helps you discover resort beaches on a map and record your visits and memories. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App collects, how it is used, and your rights.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information We Do NOT Collect

The App does **not** collect:

- Name, email address, phone number
- Account information (the App has no login feature)
- Precise location (GPS)
- Contacts
- Payment information
- Any other personally identifiable information

### 2.2 Information Stored Locally on Your Device

The following information that you enter or select within the App is stored **only on your device**. It is never sent to our servers or third parties.

- **Visited / Want-to-go lists**: Records of beaches you've visited or want to visit
- **Memos**: Notes and comments for each spot
- **Photo references**: File paths to photos you add as memories (photos themselves are stored on your device)
- **Ratings**: 1–5 star ratings
- **Filter settings**: Display filter preferences

This data is stored only in local storage (e.g., AsyncStorage) and is deleted when you uninstall the App.

---

## 3. Permissions We Use

The App may request the following permissions:

### Photo Library (Camera Roll)

- **Purpose**: To add photos to your memories for resort beaches
- **When used**: Only when you tap "Add photo"
- **Data handling**: Selected photos are stored only on your device and are never sent elsewhere

Note: The App does **not** use location (GPS). We only display maps and do not access your current location.

---

## 4. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

### 4.1 Map Display

- **iOS**: Apple Map Kit
- **Android**: Google Maps

These services are used to display maps. Please refer to each provider's privacy policy:

- [Apple Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/)
- [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

### 4.2 Other Services

The App does **not** use analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

---

## 5. Data Retention and Deletion

- All data stored by the App exists **only on your device**.
- Uninstalling the App will delete all stored data.
- We do not have servers that store your data.

---

## 6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

---

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes through the App or by other appropriate means. Your continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
